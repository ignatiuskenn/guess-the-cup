Short Description

"Guess The Cup" is a fun and challenging game where you track the ball as the cups shuffle. Test your observation skills, beat your high score, and become a guessing master!

Features

  • - Simple and engaging cup-and-ball mechanics
  • - Progressive levels and challenges
  • - Dynamic cup-shuffling animations
  • - Score tracking to challenge your friends

Development Tools

Unity Game Engine, C# Programming Language, Visual Studio, TextMesh Pro for UI elements.
